What this does

Estimates age from the camera, enrolls faces and matches identities; optionally combines mmWave radar for respiration / heart-rate views and multimodal fusion. Research demo only—not for diagnosis.

Suggested flow

  1. Under Enrollment, pick a slot and complete capture;
  2. Tap Recognize when you need identity matching;
  3. Review radar and vitals plots below.

Deployment

The browser talks to the host running face_app.py. A Raspberry Pi can stream USB camera and radar; with USE_PI_CAMERA=1, vision algorithms still run on the host.
